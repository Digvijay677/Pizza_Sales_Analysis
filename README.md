## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing pizza sales data to uncover meaningful business insights using **SQL** and **Power BI**.  
The analysis helps stakeholders understand sales performance, customer ordering behavior, and product popularity to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem
The business aims to evaluate overall sales performance and identify trends in customer purchasing behavior.  
The primary goal is to measure revenue, order patterns, and product demand while identifying opportunities to improve sales and operational efficiency.

---

## Dataset
The dataset contains transactional pizza sales data, including:
- Order ID
- Order Date & Time
- Pizza Category & Type
- Quantity Sold
- Unit Price
- Total Price

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
EDA was conducted using SQL queries to:
- Analyze daily and monthly order trends
- Identify peak ordering hours
- Understand category-wise sales distribution
- Evaluate customer purchasing patterns

---

## Key KPIs
The following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) were calculated:

- **Total Revenue**: Sum of total price of all pizza orders  
- **Average Order Value**: Total Revenue √∑ Total Orders  
- **Total Pizzas Sold**: Sum of all quantities sold  
- **Total Orders**: Count of unique orders  
- **Average Pizzas per Order**: Total Pizzas Sold √∑ Total Orders

## Key Findings & Insights
- Peak order volumes occur during specific hours of the day  
- Certain pizza categories contribute significantly more to total revenue  
- Weekends show higher order volumes compared to weekdays  
- Average pizzas per order indicate strong group or family ordering behavior
